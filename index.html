A calming puzzle game focused on traditional Sudoku gameplay.

Relax and unwind with the timeless challenge of Sudoku. In Sudoku Time, you'll immerse yourself in solving 100 classic puzzles, thoughtfully categorized from Easy to Expert. Sharpen your mind and enjoy a peaceful puzzle-solving experience on your Playdate.

Features

  • 100 Levels: Solve puzzles across Easy, Normal, Hard, and Expert difficulties.
  • Intuitive Controls: Navigate the grid with the D-Pad and select cells using the A button.
  • Progress Tracking: Your completed levels are saved, so you can pick up where you left off.
  • Minimalist Design: A clean and simple interface keeps the focus on the puzzles.

Accessibility

  • This game is appropriate for everyone.
  • Uses the A, B, and D-Pad buttons.
  • The crank is not utilized.
  • The interface is designed for clarity.
  • Players with visual impairments may find small text challenging.
